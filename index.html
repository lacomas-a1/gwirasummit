About the Summit

Background

The Gwira Gwira Creatives Summit & Gala was conceived as a platform to address the growing need for collaboration and professional development within Africa's creative industries. With the creative sector contributing significantly to GDP across the continent, this summit aims to foster connections and innovation.

Problem Statement

Africa's creative industries often operate in silos, with limited opportunities for cross-disciplinary collaboration, professional development, and access to markets and funding. This fragmentation limits the sector's potential for growth and global competitiveness.

Objectives

  • Foster collaboration across creative disciplines
  • Provide professional development opportunities
  • Showcase African creative talent to global markets
  • Create networking platforms for creatives and investors
  • Advocate for policies supporting creative industries

Theme

"Creative Convergence: Bridging Arts, Culture, and Innovation for Sustainable Development"

Program

Day 1

Join us for a community walk to raise awareness about cancer prevention and support affected creatives. The walk will be followed by health screenings and information sessions.

Day 2

Youth Showcases & Panel Discussions

A full day of presentations, exhibitions, and discussions featuring emerging creative talent. Panel topics include digital innovation in creativity, sustainable creative businesses, and accessing global markets.

Time: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Day 3

Gala Night & Awards Ceremony

A formal celebration of creative excellence featuring award presentations, performances, and networking. Black-tie attire recommended.

Time: 6:00 PM - 11:00 PM
